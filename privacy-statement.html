Privacy Statement

At GoodCare Home Health Services, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal and health information. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information obtained through our website and in the course of providing home health care services.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Statement.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Health and medical information required for care coordination
  • Insurance and billing information
  • Information submitted through online forms, inquiries, or contact requests
  • Website usage data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited

We collect only the information necessary to provide high-quality care and to communicate effectively with patients and families.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and coordinate home health services
  • Communicate with patients, families, caregivers, and healthcare providers
  • Respond to inquiries and service requests
  • Verify eligibility, insurance coverage, and billing
  • Improve our website, services, and patient experience
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and healthcare requirements

Protection of Health Information (HIPAA Compliance)

GoodCare Home Health Services complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and other applicable privacy laws. We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your Protected Health Information (PHI) from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Access to patient information is limited to authorized personnel who require it to perform their job duties.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary and permitted by law, including:

  • With healthcare providers involved in your care
  • With insurance providers for billing and coverage verification
  • With service partners who assist in operations and are bound by confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law, regulation, or court order

All third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

Website Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality and user experience. These tools help us understand how visitors use our website and allow us to enhance performance and content. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal or health information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures
  • Receive a copy of our Notice of Privacy Practices

Requests related to medical records or privacy rights will be handled in accordance with applicable laws.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously review and improve our security practices to protect your information.

External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. GoodCare Home Health Services is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit.

Updates to This Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in laws, regulations, or business practices. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
