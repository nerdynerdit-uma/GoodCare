Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

We've compiled answers to the most common questions about GoodCare Home Health Services. If you don't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.

1. What services does GoodCare Home Health Services provide?

GoodCare Home Health Services offers a full range of in-home care services, including Skilled Nursing, Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, Home Health Aide services, Supportive Wellness and Social Services, and flexible Hourly Home Care.

2. Who qualifies for home health services?

Home health services are typically available to individuals who are homebound and require skilled care ordered by a physician. Eligibility depends on medical needs, care goals, and insurance coverage. Our team can help determine qualification during an initial assessment.

3. Are your services available throughout Southern California?

Yes. GoodCare Home Health Services proudly serves communities throughout Southern California, providing reliable, in-home care tailored to local patient needs.

4. Do you offer in-home therapy services?

Yes. We provide Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, and Speech Therapy in the comfort of your home. Therapy plans are customized to each patient's condition, goals, and recovery timeline.

5. What is the difference between Home Health Aide services and Hourly Home Care?

Home Health Aide services support personal care needs as part of a medical home health plan, under clinical supervision. Hourly Home Care offers flexible, non-skilled support by the hour, ideal for families who need part-time assistance without full-time care.

6. Are your caregivers and clinicians licensed and trained?

Yes. All care is provided by licensed clinicians and trained caregivers who meet state and federal requirements. Our team is committed to professional, compassionate, and compliant care.

7. Can services be customized to fit my schedule?

Absolutely. GoodCare Home Health Services offers flexible scheduling, including hourly care options, to fit your family's routine and evolving needs.

8. Does Medicare cover home health services?

Medicare may cover home health services when eligibility requirements are met, including the need for skilled care and a physician's order. Coverage varies by service type. Our team can help explain coverage and coordinate care accordingly.

9. How do you support patients beyond medical care?

In addition to clinical services, we offer Supportive Wellness and Social Services through licensed Medical Social Workers. These services help address emotional, social, and environmental factors that may affect recovery and overall well-being.

10. How do I get started with GoodCare Home Health Services?

Getting started is simple. Contact our team to schedule an initial consultation or in-home evaluation. We will review your needs, verify eligibility, and create a personalized care plan designed to support your health and independence.
